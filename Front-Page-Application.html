Our Fees

We believe that everyone should be able to participate and learn this wonderful art regardless of their financial situation and status. We do however, need to cover fees such as renting the hall where we practice every week and the instructor. As such, to only cover our costs, we ask that members pay a fee of:

$10 / class

Members may pay up to a month's worth of fees in advance ($40). Members who are consistent and timely with their payments for a period of 3 months will receive a complementary discount of 10% for one class. If you still find financial hardship in this, please don't hesitate to contact us and we will see if you qualify for any additional discounts.
